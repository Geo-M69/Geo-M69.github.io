ABOUT
Ward's Fine Wines is located in the heart of historic 40 acres, known today as Trolley Square in Wilmington, Delaware. Established in 1933, Wards has been a trusted purveyor of fine wines in the region for many years.

Ward's has earned a unique reputation as a friendly, knowledgeable business dedicated to offering the highest levels of personal service and expert advice. Today Ward's continues the old tradition of building a person to person customer relationship and presents a world-class selection of fine wines.
